How it works

You'll sing 4 notes — a comfy low, your lowest, a comfy high, and your highest. Hold each one steady for about a second and the app catches it automatically.

🎧 Best in a quiet room. Your voice never leaves this device — nothing is recorded or sent anywhere.

Which sounds more like the singer? This helps compare you to the right voice types — men's (Bass, Baritone, Tenor) or women's (Alto, Mezzo-Soprano, Soprano).
